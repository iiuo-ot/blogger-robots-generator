Introduction

The Blogger Robots.txt Generator is a free online utility by It Is Unique Official that helps you create optimized robots.txt files for your Blogger or Blogspot websites easily.

What is Robots.txt in Blogger?

The robots.txt file tells search engine crawlers which pages or sections of your blog to index or ignore. It’s a crucial SEO component that helps control how Google and Bing access your content.

How to Use?

  • Enter your blog’s URL in the input box above.
  • Click “Generate Robots.txt”.
  • Copy the generated code.
  • Go to Blogger Settings → Crawlers and indexing → Enable custom robots.txt → Paste & Save.

Conclusion

Using a proper robots.txt ensures that your Blogger blog is SEO-optimized, avoiding duplicate content and improving index coverage. This tool simplifies that process in seconds.
